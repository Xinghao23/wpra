Get to know our wide variety of programs, classes, and camps!

With more than 70 summer and after-school courses in coding, game dev, robotics, creative arts, and math, kids and teens will develop skills, flex creativity, and experiment with the latest tools from Adobe, Epic Games, Minecraft, Roblox, and more.

VEX High School Competition

VEX Robotics High School is an engaging and dynamic educational program designed to inspire and challenge students in the field of robotics. Aimed at high school students, this program combines hands-on learning, teamwork, and problem-solving skills to foster a passion for science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

VEX Middle School Competition

VEX Robotics Middle School is an engaging and educational program designed to introduce students in middle school to the exciting world of robotics. Through hands-on learning and collaboration, this program sparks students' interest in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) while fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Introduction to VEX Robotics

Introduction to VEX Robotics is a program designed to prepare students who sparks interest in robotics. Students will build a robot over the course of 13 classes, combining basic building, design, and programming concepts to foster their STEM Skills.

Introduction to VEX Coding
